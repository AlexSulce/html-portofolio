Favourite animes

Naruto

Naruto is a show I used to watch all the time when I was younger because it used to air in the albanian channels. It is about a young ninja named Naruto (duh) who has a nine-tailed demon fox inside of him that rampaged through his village, and had to be sealed inside of him. The village hates him cause he houses the demon that claimed their loved ones lives, so he wants to become a strong ninja and the village leader in order for the people to love him.

Hunter X Hunter

Hunter X Hunter is about a young light hearted and cheerful boy named Gon Freecs. Gon is from a small island where he grew up with his aunt Mito, because his father left him in the village in order to pursue his career as a Hunter. Hunters are physically gifted individuals who are part of the Hunter organization, which selects these individuals through brutal challenges, and embarks said members on dangerous missions. We see Gons journey through the Hunter's exam, and the friendships he makes along the way.



Favourite shows

Breaking Bad

Breaking Bad shows the story of Walter White, a chemistry teacher who is immensly overqualified for his highschool job. Walter finds out he has lung cancer, and us such he takes on the alias of Heisenberg and start making crystal meth in order to make a good amount of money in order to keep his family well compensated in case of his death.

Better Call Saul

Better Call Saul is a prequel to Breaking Bad, where we see the story of Walter's lawyer, Saul Goodman (original name if James McGill), before he became the "Criminal lawyer" that he is know as in Breaking Bad.
